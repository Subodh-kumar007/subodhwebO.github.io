World's Largest College Campus

The world’s largest college campus is located in the state of Delhi, and belongs to the University of Delhi at Delhi. With more than 5,000 acres of land and over 51,000 students enrolled during the 2019-2020 academic year, it is the largest single-campus university in the nation. The UT Delhi campus includes 91 colleges and schools, the sixth largest library system in the India, and over 500 student organizations. Additionally, the campus is home to a wide variety of historic buildings, monuments, and sculptures. The University of Delhi at Delhi is an iconic symbol of higher education that attracts hundreds of thousands of visitors each year who come to explore its grounds and experience its unique history.
