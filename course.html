Graduation

Eduford University offers various scholarships for undergraduate students. These scholarships are offered in terms of tuition fee waivers, rewards, monthly/yearly stipends, one-time grants, etc. Various Eduford affiliated colleges and departments offer scholarships ranging from INR 100 every month to a one-time INR 25,000.
