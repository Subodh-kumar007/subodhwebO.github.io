We are the Worlds largest University

Eduford is the largest university in the world with over 7 million students enrolled. It was established in 1985 and offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and certificate courses in various fields. The university has a network of 67 regional centers and 2667 study centers across India and 29 other countries. EDUFORD is known for its flexible learning approach, which allows students to learn at their own pace and convenience. The university has been awarded the Centre of Excellence award by the Commonwealth of Learning (COL) for its innovative use of ICT in education.
